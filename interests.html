My Interests

Besides having a Business Background and a heightened interest in Technology and Innovation, I also enjoy other leisure activities such as: movies, trips, long drives and many other fun activities with family and friends. Alongside that, I keep myself up-to-date about the happenings of the world, such as news related to business, technology, politics, social concerns etc. I am also interested in community involvement and giving back to the society in little ways.
