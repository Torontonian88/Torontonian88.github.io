About Me

As you all know from my preview introduction, I am a Software Developer/Tester in training.

Which is why, when this opportunity to become a participant of the NPower program presented itself, I leaped at the chance to gain a better understanding of the world of technology.

If I am to describe myself in a few words, I would say that I am a technology enthusiast armed with a Business Background. Therefore, I would like to consider myself the best of both worlds.
